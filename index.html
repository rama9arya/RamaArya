Profile

Researcher in Yoga and Integrative Health specializing in the psychophysiology of Prāṇāyāma, Mind-Body Typology (Prakṛti), and Āyurveda-based interventions for chronic disorders. Experienced in EEG-based research, cardiac autonomic function testing, and clinical Yoga protocols. Studied Vyāsa’s Commentary on the Pātañjala Yoga Sūtra under the guidance of Swami Vidyapradānanda, integrating classical insights with scientific perspectives. Deep interest in allied textual traditions such as Āgama, Vedānta, Upaniṣad, and Tantra.
